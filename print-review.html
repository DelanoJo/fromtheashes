Women on Weights

This empowering program is designed specifically for women who want to build strength, confidence, and community through weight training.

What You'll Experience:

  • Safe, progressive strength training techniques
  • Myth-busting education about women and weights
  • Supportive group environment
  • Programs tailored for all fitness levels
  • Focus on functional strength and bone health

Women on Weights Notes:

Prenatal & Postnatal Fitness

Specialized training to support you through pregnancy and recovery, helping you maintain strength, prepare for childbirth, and recover safely postpartum.

Program Features:

  • Trimester-specific exercises
  • Core and pelvic floor focus
  • Safe modifications for every stage
  • Preparation for labor and delivery
  • Postpartum recovery protocols
  • Mental health support through movement

Prenatal/Postnatal Program Notes:

Senior Strength & Mobility

Age is just a number! This program focuses on maintaining and improving strength, balance, flexibility, and independence for active aging.

Fall Prevention

Balance and stability exercises to reduce fall risk.

Functional Strength

Exercises for daily tasks like carrying groceries.

Joint Health

Low-impact movements to maintain mobility.
